# ⏱️ Speed Run Relay — Timer & Transitions Guide

*For the facilitator. This is your run-of-show for the race.*

---

## Recommended Timer Tools

| Tool | Link | Best For |
|------|------|----------|
| **TimerTab** | [timertab.com](https://www.timertab.com/) | Full-screen countdown in a browser tab — project it on screen |
| **Google Timer** | Google "5 minute timer" | Quick and simple, has an alarm sound |
| **Stagetimer** | [stagetimer.io](https://stagetimer.io/) | Pro-level — set up the full sequence in advance |
| **Phone timer** | Your phone | Works in a pinch — prop it up where everyone can see |

💡 **Pro tip:** If you're running this on Zoom/Google Meet, share your timer tab so remote participants can see the countdown.

## Announcements Script

Copy-paste these or read them aloud. The energy matters — this should feel like a real race.

### Pre-Race (2:00 before start)

> 🎙️ "Alright everyone, this is the Speed Run Relay. In the next 22 minutes, your team is going to build a complete product — from blank folder to polished prototype with documentation.
>
> Quick rules:
> - Only one person works at a time
> - When your leg is done, post EVERYTHING to your team channel
> - The next person can't start until they have the handoff
> - The clock does not stop
>
> Architects — you're up first. Everyone else: watch, cheer, and get ready."

### 🟢 Leg 1 Start

> 🎙️ "ARCHITECTS — YOUR TIME STARTS NOW! Five minutes on the clock. Build that foundation!"

### Leg 1 — 1:00 Warning

> 🎙️ "ONE MINUTE LEFT for Architects! Start sharing your CLAUDE.md and wireframe!"

### Leg 1 — Time's Up

> 🎙️ "TIME! Architects, post everything to your team channel RIGHT NOW."

### 🟡 Transition to Leg 2 (0:30)

> 🎙️ "Builders — grab the files from your team channel. You've got 30 seconds to get set up. Your clock starts soon."

### 🟢 Leg 2 Start

> 🎙️ "BUILDERS — GO! Seven minutes! Turn that wireframe into a working prototype!"

### Leg 2 — 2:00 Warning

> 🎙️ "TWO MINUTES! Your prototype should be working by now. Test it, fix it, ship it!"

### Leg 2 — Time's Up

> 🎙️ "TIME! Post your prototype NOW. Designers are waiting!"

### 🟡 Transition to Leg 3 (0:30)

> 🎙️ "Designers — get the prototype. Open it in your browser. Take a 'before' screenshot. You're up in 30 seconds."

### 🟢 Leg 3 Start

> 🎙️ "DESIGNERS — GO! Five minutes to make it beautiful!"

### Leg 3 — 1:00 Warning

> 🎙️ "ONE MINUTE! Take your 'after' screenshot and post!"

### Leg 3 — Time's Up

> 🎙️ "TIME! Designers, post your before/after and the updated file!"

### 🟡 Transition to Leg 4 (0:30)

> 🎙️ "PMs — this is the final leg. Grab the polished prototype. You're closing this out."

### 🟢 Leg 4 Start

> 🎙️ "PMs — GO! Five minutes! Write the docs that ship this product! THIS IS THE FINAL LEG!"

### Leg 4 — 1:00 Warning

> 🎙️ "ONE MINUTE! Package it up — release notes, product description, v2 ideas. GO!"

### Leg 4 — Time's Up

> 🎙️ "THAT'S TIME! THE RACE IS OVER! 🏁 Hands off the keyboard! Every team post your final package NOW."

### 🏁 Presentations

> 🎙️ "Incredible. Let's see what you built. Each team: you get 30 seconds. Show the prototype, read your tagline, share one v2 idea. Team Alpha — you're first."

### 🏆 Awards

> 🎙️ "The scores are in... [dramatic pause]
>
> 🥇 Fastest Ship goes to... [TEAM]!
> 🎨 Best Design goes to... [TEAM]!
> 🧠 Most Creative goes to... [TEAM]!
>
> And the overall winner of the Speed Run Relay...
>
> 🏆 [TEAM]!"

### ⚡ Twist Round (Optional)

> 🎙️ "Wait — we're not done. I just got a message from a customer. There's a critical bug. Check your channels — the bug report just dropped. ENTIRE teams work together this time. First team to fix it and post proof gets 10 bonus points. GO!"

## Music Suggestions (Optional)

Background music sets the tone. Play it quietly during the legs:

- **During legs:** Upbeat lo-fi or electronic focus music (search "hackathon playlist" on Spotify)
- **During transitions:** A short countdown sound effect or buzzer
- **During presentations:** Something triumphant — think awards show walk-up music
- **Twist round:** Intense — action movie soundtrack energy

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| A team's handoff didn't post in time | Give them 30 extra seconds, note the overtime on the scorecard |
| Someone's Claude Code crashes | They can restart and continue — the files are still on disk |
| A team member is stuck and can't finish | Their teammates can coach from the sidelines (verbal only — no touching the keyboard) |
| Teams finish at wildly different times | Early finishers can refine their work, but no new features after time is called |
| Not enough people for teams of 3-4 | Run it as a "Solo Relay" — each person does all 4 legs, competing individually |
