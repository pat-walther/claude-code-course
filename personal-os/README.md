# Build Your Personal OS

A lesson-based course that teaches AgVend's GTM team how to build their own Personal OS inside Claude Co-work — an AI-powered productivity system that deeply understands who you are, what you do, and what matters to you.

## How to Use

1. Open Claude Co-work (Claude Desktop app)
2. Create a new project and grant it access to a folder on your machine
3. Pick a lesson from the list below
4. Tell Claude: "Read this file and teach me the lesson. You're the teacher — go one step at a time and wait for my responses."
5. Follow along. Every lesson ends with something real in your Personal OS.

## Lessons

| # | Title | What you'll build |
|---|-------|-------------------|
| 0 | Why Claude Co-work changes everything | Your Co-work project folder |
| 1 | Why Markdown & setting up your Personal OS | Folder structure: Knowledge/, Tasks/, BACKLOG.md |
| 2 | Build your Constitution | Constitution.md — your values, beliefs, and principles |
| 3 | Build your Business context | Business.md — your role, accounts, and competitive landscape |
| 4 | Define your Goals | GOALS.md — priorities by timeframe |
| 5 | Understanding AGENTS.md & wiring it together | AGENTS.md that connects everything |
| 6 | Daily workflow | Backlog processing, task management, daily rhythms |
| 7 | Memory, context, and keeping your OS alive | A system that grows with you |
| 8 | Skills — automate the upkeep | Meeting debrief skill + your own custom skill |

## Templates

Pre-built files to get you started are in `templates/`:
- `AGENTS.md` — AgVend-framed template with placeholders
- `agvend-context.md` — Pre-filled company context
- `BACKLOG.md` — Empty backlog with instructions

## Requirements

- Claude Desktop app with Co-work enabled (Pro, Max, Team, or Enterprise plan)
- A folder on your machine to use as your Personal OS workspace
- No coding experience required
