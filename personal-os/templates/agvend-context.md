# AgVend Company Context

## What AgVend Does
AgVend provides digital solutions for agricultural retailers, helping them modernize their customer experience through technology. The platform enables ag retailers to build stronger relationships with their grower customers through digital tools.

## Products & Platform
- **E-commerce**: Online ordering and product catalog for ag inputs (seed, crop protection, fertilizer)
- **Digital Marketing**: Tools for ag retailers to reach and engage their grower customers
- **Customer Engagement**: Loyalty programs, communications, and relationship management
- **Data & Insights**: Analytics and reporting on customer behavior and purchasing patterns

## Who We Serve
- **Primary customers**: Agricultural retailers (co-ops, independent dealers, regional chains)
- **End users**: Growers/farmers who buy from those retailers
- **Our value proposition**: We help ag retailers compete in a digital world without losing the personal relationships that define their business

## Go-to-Market Team
- **Sales**: New business acquisition and expansion within existing accounts
- **Customer Success**: Onboarding, adoption, retention, and growth
- **Marketing**: Demand generation, content, events, and brand
- **Leadership**: Strategy, partnerships, and market expansion

## Market Context
- Agricultural retail is consolidating — smaller retailers need digital tools to compete
- Growers increasingly expect digital buying experiences similar to consumer e-commerce
- Competitors range from legacy ag software providers to general-purpose e-commerce platforms
- Relationships and trust are the core differentiator in ag retail — technology should enhance these, not replace them

## Key Metrics
- Retailer adoption and engagement
- Grower digital adoption rates
- Revenue growth and expansion within accounts
- Customer retention and satisfaction
