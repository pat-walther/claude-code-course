# Claude Code for Product Managers

Interactive lessons designed to be pasted into Claude Code. Each lesson guides you to a "magic moment" — the point where your mental model shifts and you realize what's possible.

## How to Use

1. Open Claude Code in your project directory
2. Pick a lesson from the list below
3. Tell Claude: "Read lessons/01-course-overview.md and follow its instructions to teach me. You're the teacher — go one step at a time and wait for my responses."
4. Follow along. Every lesson ends with something working.

## Lessons

### Day 1 — Get Comfortable with Claude Code
- `01-course-overview.md` — Welcome to Claude Code: what it is, how it works, shortcuts, and your three superpowers
- `02-first-site.md` — Build your personal website and publish it live on the internet
- `03-copy-and-customize.md` — Iterate with pro design techniques, then publish and share

### Day 2 — Bring In Your Context
- `04-bring-your-context.md` — Feed Claude your product files, specs, and create your memory file
- `05-leverage-plans.md` — Plan before you build for dramatically better results
- `06-brainstorm-features.md` — Brainstorm features, build prototypes in parallel, compare and combine

### Day 3 — Craft Beautiful Designs
- `08-skills-overview.md` — Teach Claude your workflows with reusable skills
- `09-style-guide.md` — Create your style guide so everything matches your brand
- `10-recreate-designs.md` — Recreate any design you see on the internet
- `11-analyze-and-discover.md` — Discover what to build next from user feedback and product analysis

### Day 4 — Automate Your Whole Job
- `12-marketing-assets.md` — Create diagrams, social graphics, and animated demos
- `13-brand-voice.md` — Create your brand voice + write release notes and stakeholder updates
- `14-competitive-analysis.md` — Analyze the competition + write product guides
- `15-design-for-agents.md` — Make your product discoverable by AI and search
- `16-openclaw.md` — Your AI chief of staff + build your own custom skill

### Day 5 — Bring Prototypes to Production
- `17-shipping-safely.md` — How to release features without breaking everything
- `18-github-and-cicd.md` — GitHub — where your product lives online
- `19-publish-your-app.md` — Publish your app with automatic quality checks
- `20-share-with-team.md` — Share your setup with your team + course wrap-up

### Day 6 — Shareouts & Q&A
No lesson files — this is your day to present what you've built!

## Games

Alternative entry points that teach Claude Code through gameplay.

| Game | Format | Players | Time | What You Learn |
|------|--------|---------|------|----------------|
| [Baby Claude](games/baby-claude.md) | Terminal RPG | Solo | 10 min | The basics through 5 level-ups |
| [Escape Room](games/escape-room.md) | Puzzle chain | Solo | 15 min | Decoding, data analysis, debugging, building |
| [Choose Your Prototype](games/choose-your-prototype.md) | Branching narrative | Solo | 10 min | Prototyping under pressure with 3 paths |
| [Speed Run Relay](games/speed-run-relay.md) | Team relay race | 3-4 per team | 25 min | Full build pipeline as a team |

## Requirements
- Claude Code installed ([claude.ai/download](https://claude.ai/download))
- No coding experience required — this course is designed for non-technical product managers
- A web browser and curiosity
